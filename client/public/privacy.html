1. Introduction

Welcome to Bloom ("we," "our," or "us"). Bloom is a pregnancy companion mobile application featuring Ivy, an AI assistant designed to provide educational information, emotional support, and organizational tools throughout your pregnancy journey.

This Privacy Policy explains how Zelkz LLC ("Company") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the Bloom mobile application (the "App"). By using Bloom, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Bloom, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data when you sign in
  • Pregnancy Information: Due date, baby's name, baby's sex
  • Health Logs: Mood, symptoms, weight, and notes you choose to record
  • AI Conversations: Questions you ask Ivy and the context you provide
  • Appointments: Healthcare appointments you schedule in the app

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Deliver personalized pregnancy guidance and respond to your questions through Ivy AI
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communication: Send important updates about the service

4. Third-Party Services

Bloom uses trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication
  • OpenAI: Powers Ivy's AI responses
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google/Apple: Sign-in authentication

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and regular security reviews.

6. Your Rights
